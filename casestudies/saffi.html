Creating an online presence for a clothing boutique

Brief

Saffi boutique is an independent fashion house located in Blakemere village. They previously only had an online presence on Facebook. Their goal was to bring more people into their shop through their new website.

Visit the website

Research

I used the search term "ladies fashion boutique" to look at competitor's sites and see what worked for them.


  • Nav must be clear to use and showcase all the clothing categories.
  • Striking hero image, with CTA on top will encourage users to click.
  • Textures look stylish, overlayed with images to help build the brand.
  • Grid layout looks clean.
  • Gold gives a luxurious feel to a brand.
Mood board

User personas

From conversations with the client and user reviews on Saffi Boutique's Facebook page, my team was able to create the following insights:


  • Users like to keep up to date with the latest fashion trends.
  • Their key pain point was bad customer service.
  • Key empathy findings were, users needed to shop somewhere that has unique fashion pieces and the environment is family friendly.
