Creating an online presence for a clothing boutique

Brief

Saffi Boutique only had an online presence on Facebook, until they requested a brochure website that was strictly non ecommerce. Their goal was to get people to visit their shop for events and personal shopping.

Data

As their existing client base access the boutique on their smart phones, I felt it was important for the site to function well across all devices, so I designed it mobile first. Findings from heat maps showed users normally click a call to action in the hero section, so this led me to place the "new in" button on the hero image. Using scroll maps, we found on our other sites users clicked off at the end of the product section. This made me come to the decision to place buckets at the bottom of the page, so users had somewhere to navigate to next.
