Entice visitors to book a home visit for a blinds quote

Brief

Divise a website that showcases all variations of blinds.

Visit the website

Problem

Previously the client had an outdated website that wasn't generating much traffic.

  • Website looks old.
  • Not enough pages on the site to draw in leads.
  • Lack of product information.
  • No booking form.

    • Solution

  • Using the logo, I created a style guide.
  • I mapped user flows to help me understand how users navigated throughout the website.
  • Created content that encourages users to convert.
User flow

Findings

  • 58% searches from mobile phones.
  • 26% searches from desktop.
  • Predominantly female market.
  • Influencing buying decisions include: price, variety of colours and variety of styles.
  • The main factors that encourage customers to use a certain blind supplies are: price, good reviews and good customer service.
  • If price is a factor the main things that encourage a purchase are: Home visits, guarantees and material sample blocks.

Sitemap

Looking at top keywords, Roller blinds, venetian blinds and window shutters I ensured they had their own pages as these are what visitors are most likely to land on from Google.
