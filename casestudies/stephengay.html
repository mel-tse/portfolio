Redesigning an existing website to generate more leads.

Brief

The client wishes to have their website updated to bring in more business.

Visit the website

Problems

  • Poor user flow
  • Pricing isn't transparent
  • Current website isn't responsive

    • Solution

  • From my discover phase, I found one of the users's needs were price transparancy, to solve this, I divised a pricing plan.
  • To combat the poor user flow. I placed an "arrange an appointment" button in every hero section and placed the contact form at the bottom of the page.
  • I ensured the site was responsive accross all devices.
User personas

Findings

  • 58% searches from mobile phones.
  • 28% searches from desktop.
  • 14% searches from tablet.
  • Main key search terms: 'funerals', 'Prepaid funeral plans' 'funeral costs' and 'cremations'
  • Retired people are most likeley to have a funeral plan
  • Web design conversions: Call, Email, Purchase
  • Pain points - lack of funeral planning knowledge, funeral costs are not transparent.

Sitemap

From looking at their current website, I was able to compare it with other funeral sites. This was to see if they offered any aditional services. On their current website they highlighted they specialise in memorial services. From my findings, we were able to sell the client an extra page.
