Encouraging users to book tyre fittings online

Brief

Create a website that will encourage user's to book their tyre fittings online.

Visit the website

Problem

From looking at scroll map data from our Tyre Sector Research. It was clear users didnâ€™t want to spend too long on the website, they often bounced, if the process was too long or complex.

  • I used mouseflow to see where visitors are clicking on one of our sites, mrtyre.com.
  • The "Find my tyres" filter within the hero section had a high conversion rate.

    • Solution

  • I designed a step by step process with images, this made the page scroll down to the search feature.
  • I also implemented a component that allows the user to enter their registration or tyre size. This initiates a widget so they can buy their tyres online, this will let them book their appointment if they've already brought the tyres.
Component designs

Findings

  • 58% searches from mobile phones.
  • 33% searches from desktop.
  • Buyers want the decision to be quick and easy with the least hassle and return the most value.
  • Tyre fittings are essential, despite the quality of rubber lasting longer.
  • Pain points - modern tyre materials are lasting longer, there are competitors that import tyres from overseas offering cheaper services.
