About Me.

Hey I'm Mel. I’m a passionate Digital Designer, with experience in Front-End Development.

As a Designer and Coder I understand that a great user experience should look good and work even better. I’m passionate about creating digital products, from sketching the initial wireframes, to coding the finished product.

More about me

Portfolio Intro.

I’ve spent the last year working at Adtrak, one of Nottingham’s most well known digital agencies, where I’ve delivered projects end-to-end for a multitude of SME businesses across the UK.

During my time at Adtrak, I’ve been able to grow my skills in all areas of web design, from leading UX research though to designing and front-end coding of digital experiences. I focus on a user-centered approach, whilst keeping in line with design principles.

I am proud to be a part of a multidisciplinary team, working closely with talented individuals from a variety of marketing specialities. Everyday I learn something new, as I collaborate with my colleagues to get the best results for our clients and their users.
